Definition of centimeter

A centimeter (cm) is a decimal fraction of the meter, the international standard unit of length, approximately equivalent to 39.37 inches.

Definition of inch

An inch is a unit of length or distance in a number of systems of measurement, including in the US Customary Units and British Imperial Units. One inch is defined as 1⁄12 of a foot and is therefore 1⁄36 of a yard. According to the modern definition, one inch is equal to 25.4 mm exactly.

Centimeter to inches formula and conversion factor

To calculate a centimeter value to the corresponding value in inch, just multiply the quantity in centimeter by 0.39370078740157 (the conversion factor).

Centimeter to inches formulae

Inches = Centimeters * 0.39370078740157

The factor 0.39370078740157 is the result from the division 1/2.54 (Inch definition). So, a better formula is

Inches = Centimeters / 2.54
